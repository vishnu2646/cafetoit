Discover

Café Toit is the first rooftop cafe in hosur and till date is the only one. It is built on 2000sq.ft space to give customer spacious look. The tables are arranged in such way considering the privacy of the customers, for kids to play around.

We have also opened up the ambiance for business meetings, short working hours, gatherings, small parties etc. with free Wi-Fi connections.
